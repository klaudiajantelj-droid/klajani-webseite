Why KLAJANI Safety

Operational disruptions, technical failures and safety incidents create significant financial, reputational and legal consequences — often from vulnerabilities that were visible long before the incident.

KLAJANI Safety works with owners, operators and management teams to identify and address exposure before it becomes a problem. Our approach is independent, discreet and calibrated to each property's operational context — not a product checklist, not a compliance formality.

Our Services

A structured path from
observation to protection

KLAJANI Safety engagements are structured as a progressive advisory process. Products and technical solutions are considered only where they serve the strategy — never the starting point.

01

Safety Quick Check

First impression. Real findings.

A complimentary on-site walkthrough that surfaces immediate operational and infrastructure vulnerabilities. No preparation required. No commitment to proceed.

  • 15–30 minutes on-site
  • Verbal findings debrief
  • Confidential — no written record unless requested

Available for hotels, gyms, restaurants, yachts and commercial properties

02

Safety Intelligence Report

The strategic overview owners trust.

A comprehensive assessment delivering a structured, actionable view of your property's risk profile. Designed for owners, operators, investors and insurers who require documentation.

  • Full risk identification and scoring
  • Technical and operational review
  • Photographic documentation
  • Executive summary and action recommendations
  • Calibrated for board and investor-level review
03

Protection Concept

Strategy before solutions.

A tailored roadmap that defines priorities, evaluates options and maps implementation sequences — independent of any supplier or technology. The foundation for informed investment decisions.

  • Derived from the Intelligence Report
  • Phased implementation structure
  • Vendor-neutral recommendations
04

Annual Safety Review

Sustained protection, not a one-off exercise.

Ongoing reassessment of operational risk as your property evolves. Infrastructure changes, new operations, staff transitions and emerging vulnerabilities are evaluated and addressed systematically.

  • Annual on-site assessment
  • Updated risk scoring
  • Progress review against prior recommendations
